About Us

Something About Us

Ananthapuri Security and HR Services is a trusted and reliable provider of security and human resources solutions in Thiruvananthapuram, Kerala. We are a franchise of Command Securitas and HR Services, a leading company in the industry with over 20 years of experience and expertise.
We offer a range of services to meet the diverse needs of our clients, including :

  • Security guards for events
  • Security guards for properties
  • Security supervisors

Our security personnel are well-trained, professional, and courteous, and are equipped with the latest technology and equipment to ensure the safety and security of our clients and their assets.
At Ananthapuri Security and HR Services, we are committed to delivering high-quality services at competitive prices. We value customer satisfaction and loyalty, and we strive to exceed your expectations. Contact us today to find out how we can help you with your security and HR needs.
